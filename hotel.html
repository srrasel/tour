Kashmir is one of the most fascinating places in India and the world. It is known for its surreal beauty that has no comparison. Many famous poets have written about the beauty of this place. Kashmir is also given the status of being the paradise on Earth. This valley is surrounded by mountains with snow-covered peaks, beautiful flowers, lakes and beautiful people. The quiet place is an absolute dream for people looking for a vacation in a peaceful spot. This paradise on Earth is on every traveler’s list. It has that bewitching power of making one fall in love with it. Since the place is so intriguing it lures many tourists every year hence there are many staying options that have mushroomed here. Kashmir hotels can be easily found and booked as per one’s needs and requirements.

  • Key Info About Kashmir
  • A must-visit for mountain lovers
  • Famous for the tulip festival
  • A perfect destination for honeymooners
  • Home to beautiful houseboats
  • Types Of Accommodation
Because of the big footfall of tourists in Kashmir, there are multiple staying options here. People, depending on their interests, can choose and have a great time. Hotels - There are several hotels in Kashmir offering you the most amazing stay and great hospitality. These hotels vary in budgets and guests can choose as per their likes and needs. They offer a comfortable stay and a great view for you to fall in love with every single day. Cottages - Kashmir, along with hotels, also have few cottages. These cottages again depending on the budget offer various kinds of amenities. From the most luxurious to the most basic, you will find them here. Houseboats - Kashmir is very popular for its houseboats. It is one of a kind experience that every tourist must-have. Choose your stay in one of the houseboats that consist of intricate wooden work with foliage and other beautiful things. They are warm, cozy and again some are very fancy whereas some are the most basic ones. Dining & Food Experience When it comes to food, Kashmir will make you fall in love with the local cuisine that one can try here. If you are a nonvegetarian then you are definitely in food heaven. There are many restaurants and small eating joints where you can go to try authentic local cuisine. The hotels in Kashmir have in -house restaurants with the best chefs who offer an amazing dining experience to their guests. They have a well-curated menu offering delicious food. Some hotels also offer various kinds of cuisines from all around the world. When you are dining here make sure you try these local dishes
  • Rogan Josh
  • Modur Pulov
  • Matschgand
  • Yakhni
  • Dum Olav
  • Kashmiri Muji Gaad
  • Goshtaba
  • Rishta
Popular Areas To Stay When deciding to book hotels in Kashmir, choose one of the best places to stay at which are popular and have immense beauty. They are 1. Srinagar - Srinagar is one of the most popular places to stay in Kashmir. It is beautiful and is home to the beautiful Dal Lake. It is a peaceful place to stay where one can enjoy their vacation to the fullest. 2. Gulmarg - This town hill station is very popular for its unparalleled beauty. Gulmarg is especially very popular among ski lovers as it is one of the most popular places for it. With snow-covered mountains, valley flowers, deep ravines and so much more, it is truly one of the most beautiful places in India. 3. Sonmarg - With majestic glaciers and serene lakes, Sonmarg is another popular place to stay at when looking for staying accommodation in Kashmir. This is also a popular attraction among tourists and deserves special attention. Shopping & Entertainment Kashmir is one of the most pristine destinations and offers a surreal experience to the tourists. There are a lot of things that one can do here. Popular Markets - Kashmir is definitely worth the hype when it comes to shopping. There is the famous Srinagar market along with many smaller areas with a few shops where one can shop from. People can buy Kashmiri handicrafts, wooden decorative items, dry fruits, clothes, carpets and much more from here. Sightseeing Places - There are many places to visit when in Kashmir. From Pahalgam to Dal lake in Srinagar, Mughal Gardens, Sonamarg, Gulmarg, Shalimar Bagh there is a lot to see and explore here. Getting Around In Kashmir When it comes to going around and exploring Kashmir, there are different modes of transportations that can be used. They are Local Bus - There are local buses that one can board to cover a longer distance like from Gulmarg to Sonamarg. These buses run at a particular time and are convenient for such long journeys. Taxi - Another popular mode of transportation is hiring a cab to explore the places. They are easily available and one of the convenient options and very comfortable. Mini Buses - Minibuses are very common in Kashmir and widely popular. They are also known as matadors. They are operational throughout the city and have a particular route. Autos - Autos are also run throughout the city and are easily available to cover smaller distances. Special Experiences There are various activities and experiences that one can have here in Kashmir Valley. Some of the experiences are completely offbeat and incredible. Have an offbeat experience of shopping from a shikara. They are floating markets on the Dal Lake which can be reached via a shikara only. Some hotels in Kashmir also offer trekking and hiking in the nearby area. There are so many beautiful routes depending on all difficulty levels which are very popular among adventure lovers. Some amazing hotels offer the experience of skiing in this fabulous place and along with a ride in the second-highest and second-longest car cable. Not just this, some of the properties also arrange for a skiing guide for you to take lessons. Photographers could enjoy capturing stunning views all day long.
