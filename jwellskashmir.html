Overview

The 04N/05D Jewels in Kashmir Tour designed by the expert travel team of Tour My India is a wonderful combination of sightseeing and adventure. The tour package covers sightseeing at the popular travel destinations of Jammu & Kashmir- Srinagar, Pahalgam, Gulmarg & Sonmarg.
In this Kashmir holiday package, you will enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake in Srinagar, visit the Betaab Valley in Pahalgam, take 8 minutes Gondola Cable Car Ride in Gulmarg & enjoy a pony ride to Thajiwas Glacier, besides doing a lot of other things.

Welcome to Srinagar, The Lake City. On arrival at the Srinagar airport, you will be welcomed & received by Tour My India representative/driver. Once you board your vehicle, you will proceed to the houseboat.
After check in, proceed to sightseeing of Srinagar. Visit the famous Mughal Gardens, Nishat Bagh (the garden of pleasure), Shalimar Bagh (abode of love), the Chasme Shahi Garden & Shankaracharya Temple.

After breakfast at the houseboat, leave for a day excursion to Pahalgam. Located at 2,133 mts, it is also known as â€˜The valley of Shepherdsâ€™ or â€˜Trekker's Paradiseâ€™. Pahalgam is the most famous place for Indian Film Industry.

Enroute visit Saffron fields, Awantipur ruins (1100 yrs old temple of Lord Vishnu built by King Awantiverma), see the beautiful countryside, and lots of rice fields.

Arrive at Pahalgam and depart for an excursion trip to Chandanwadi (on your own), the starting point of Amarnath Yatra & Betab valley (on your own). Located on the way to Chandanwari, the valley was named Betaab after the Bollywood movie by the same name! Earlier known as Hajan Valley, after the release of the movie, it was renamed as a major part of the movie was shot here.

Post lunch (on your own), or as time permits, proceed for a trip to Aru Valley (on your own). Aru Valley is a tourist spot located almost 11 km from Pahalgam noted for its scenic meadows, and mountains, and a base camp for trekking to the Kolahoi Glacier and Tarsar Lake. The village lies on the left bank of the Aru river, which is a tributary of the Lidder River. All these activities will be conducted at your own expense.

In the evening, return to Srinagar for overnight stay at the hotel.

Note : In Pahalgam outside vehicles are not allowed for sightseeing. Only local taxis can roam around within Pahalgam city. All outside vehicles will pick up & drop from Hotel

After breakfast at the hotel, leave for a day excursion to Gulmarg, the drive past colorful villages and rice fields gives you an insight of the rich cultural past of Kashmir. Gulmarg is one of the most beautiful hill stations in this world, and is also called Meadows of Flowers.

It has the worldâ€™s highest golf course (worldâ€™s highest), and Gulmarg is also famous for winter sports like skiing. But the most popular thing to include in the tour package in Kashmir is the Gondola Cable Car Ride to Khilanmarg. The 08 minutes of ropeway will take you past colorful shepherd hutments to the lower ridge of the Apharwat Peak. From here you can click lots of photos of the nearby mountain ranges that stand tall like sentinels, guarding the rich natural beauty of Kashmir valley. We descend back to Gulmarg base after an hour.

After breakfast at the houseboat/hotel, leave for a day excursion to Sonmarg, which is the most beautiful drive from Srinagar. On the way you can stop at many beautiful spots. Continue driving to Sonamarg 2690 Mtrs (Meadow of Gold). One can ride on horse to visit Thajiwas Glacier (on Pony) where snow remains round the year and Sonamarg is known as Gateway of Ladakh.

In the evening return to Srinagar for overnight stay at hotel.

Srinagar Airport Drop

Begin the last day of your tour package for Kashmir with a delish breakfast in your hotel at Srinagar. Post breakfast, you will be transferred to the Srinagar Airport. Board your flight for respective hub with memorable experiences of Tour My India.

Important Information : Due to strict security, you are requested to leave from the hotel at least 4 hours before the departure time. You will have to pass 4 security barriers before reaching the airport. This often leads to long traffic jams right outside the airport.
